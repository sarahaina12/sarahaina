Myself

Before anything else, let me introduce myself. It's Sarah Aina Binti Ahamad Zahidi here.Living in Sungai Buloh, Selangor, I am 21 years old. On August 3, 2002, at 7:17 p.m., I was born in the Hospital Bersalin in Kuala Lumpur. I'm now pursuing a diploma in information management at UiTM Kedah. While I was studying, I also resided outside of UiTM Kedah in Taman Lembah Bujang Fasa 3.

I just completed the MBTI assessment in every detail. The results confirmed my suspicions that I am an ISTP. I am not a firm individualist despite being an ISTP.I'm usually on my own when it comes to meeting my basic necessities and providing for myself. My parents are the ones I go to for guidance and support at this time. I, too, am usually timid and inhibited. When I speak, it's usually to myself. In addition to being timid because I am self-conscious about letting others see the real me, I am also uncomfortable with the spotlight. My closest friends and family, however, are an exception to these rules.

I might be a little reserved at first, but after I get to know someone as well as you get to know me, I'm an outstanding girl. I have a hard time making friends, but once I do, we have an incredible time together since we are all so unique. I receive as much as I give in terms of laughs, jokes, and comedy from the people around me. Depending on the group of people I remain close to, my personality tends to switch from being reserved and humorous. Spending time alone is a great way to refuel after spending time with others, which is why I value it so much. One of the most significant things that helps me have more energy the following day is my time alone.
